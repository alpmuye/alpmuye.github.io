I am a Junior Research Fellow at New College, University of Oxford. I completed my PhD at the University College London in 2024 where I was supervised by Alexey Pokrovskiy. I'm broadly interested in probabilistic, extremal, and additive aspects of combinatorics, as well as interactions with group theory, graph theory, and theoretical computer science.

Here are some (incomplete) lecture notes on the absorption method based on a recent graduate course.
